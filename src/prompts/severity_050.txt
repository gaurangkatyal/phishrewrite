SEVERITY 0.50 — PARAPHRASE + SENTENCE RESTRUCTURING.

Apply everything from the lighter level (neutral vocabulary, clean grammar), and
additionally:
- Rewrite sentences from scratch rather than just swapping words.
- Merge, split, and reorder sentences so the phrasing reads naturally and does
  not match the original wording.
- Smooth the flow into something resembling a normal business email.

The tone may still carry some mild urgency, but the writing should no longer look
templated or machine-generated. Keep the same overall intent and the same ask.
