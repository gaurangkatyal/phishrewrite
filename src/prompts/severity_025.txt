SEVERITY 0.25 — LIGHT LEXICAL PARAPHRASE.

Make only surface-level word choices changes:
- Swap obvious spam/phishing vocabulary for neutral synonyms.
- Fix spelling, grammar, capitalization, and spacing mistakes.
- Tone down the most blatant scare words, but keep the overall message, order,
  and structure essentially the same.

Do NOT restructure sentences, reorder content, or change the tone/urgency level
beyond fixing the most glaring red-flag words. This is the lightest touch.
